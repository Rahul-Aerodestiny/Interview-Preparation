9. What is the difference between a relay and a circuit breaker?

Ans: A relay senses and signals fault, while a circuit breaker actually interrupts current flow.

10. What is an RLC circuit?

Ans: A circuit consisting of a Resistor (R), Inductor (L), and Capacitor (C) connected in series or parallel.

11. Explain the concept of resonance in RLC circuits.

Ans: Resonance occurs when inductive reactance equals capacitive reactance, maximizing current.

12. What is a synchronous motor?

Ans: A motor that runs at constant speed in synchronism with supply frequency.

13. Difference between three-phase and single-phase systems?

Ans: Three-phase provides more power, efficiency, and smoother operation compared to single-phase.

14. What are semiconductors used for in electrical systems?

Ans: They are used in rectifiers, transistors, diodes, and power electronics for switching and control.

15. What is the function of a capacitor?

Ans: A capacitor stores electrical energy in an electric field and is used in filtering, power factor correction, etc.

Ans: A rectifier converts AC to DC.

Ans: An isolator is a switch used to ensure circuit is completely de-energized for maintenance.
